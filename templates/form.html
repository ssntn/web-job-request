UITC Services

The "Attendance Record" section provides a convenient and efficient document request service for obtaining your attendance records. It encompasses both daily time records and biometric records, offering a comprehensive overview of your presence at the university. By utilizing this service, you can easily request and access accurate information regarding your attendance history. Whether you need these records for personal reference, official purposes, or any other requirement, our system ensures a streamlined process for retrieving the necessary documentation.

Manage your university accounts effortlessly with our "Account Management" service. Whether you need to create a new account, reset passwords, update information, or request technical support, we've got you covered. Supported accounts include TUP Portal, TUP Office 365, ERS (the old system), TUP Official Email, and TUP Web ERS. Take control of your digital access and enjoy a seamless experience throughout your academic or professional journey. Sign up, manage, and troubleshoot with ease. Maximize your university resources with our reliable and user-friendly service.

Experience reliable Network Installation and Repair Services tailored to your needs. Our expert team specializes in wired and wireless internet connections, as well as telephone systems. Whether you require seamless connectivity, troubleshooting, or maintenance, we've got you covered. Trust us to deliver efficient solutions and ensure your network operates at its best.

At our "ICT Equipment Management" service, we offer a range of convenient services to ensure your technology needs are met. Whether you require repairs, maintenance, or equipment borrowing, we've got you covered. Our extensive inventory includes a variety of essential devices such as desktop computers, printers, LED walls, laptops, and photocopying machines. And if you need other equipment, just let us know, as we may have additional options available upon request

Simplify your software and website management with our solutions. Install software including security apps, productivity tools like the MS Office 365 suite, or specify other applications and information systems you need. Additionally, easily publish and update information on your website. Streamline your operations and maximize your digital presence with our efficient Software and Website Management Service.
